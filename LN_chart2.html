Hubness Rank vs Influence Rank

Hubness rank (Betweenness) of a node plotted against Influence (Eigenvector). This chart is beneficial in figuring out a peer. It is good to imagine 4 quadrants - 'High Influence-High hubness', 'High Influence-Low hubness', 'Low Influence-High hubness', 'Low Influence-Low hubless' and think of how nodes are playing their role in the graph
